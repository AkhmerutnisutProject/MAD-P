Welcome to the Mastaba of Akhmerutnisut Documentation Project (MAD-P)

The Mastaba of Akhmerutnisut Documentation Project aims to study, document, and preserve the tomb (mastaba) of the Fifth Dynasty palace official Akhmerutnisut (G 2184), who lived around 2300 BCE and who was buried at Giza, to the west of the Great Pyramid.
