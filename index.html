Welcome to the Mastaba of Akhmerutnisut Documentation Project (MAD-P)

Since 2023, MAD-P has been studying the tomb of the 5th dynasty palace official Akhmerutnisut at Giza. Together with our study, we are also stabilizing and preserving this important and unusual ancient Egyptian funerary monument for the next generations. Having been affected by pollution and weather conditions the clock is ticking to make sure Akhmerutnisut's tomb will continue to stand proudly in the shadow of the three pyramids at Giza.
