Support our work

If you enjoy this project and want to help it continue, you can support us by making a donation. Even the smallest contributions help to preserve this rapidly deteriorating monument at Giza. For instance, with a donation of $25 we can pay a workman in our team a full day's wages, which is above the current minimum wage. Thank you!

Donation tiers

$1-$500 - Your name will be mentioned on our website and in public presentations as benefactor (if you so wish). You will also get first-hand updates about our ongoing fieldwork in a private group WhatsApp (during the fieldwork seasons).


$501-1000 - The above-mentioned benefits and you will also receive executive producer credit on the YouTube video we publish each fieldwork season.


+ $1000 - The above-mentioned benefits and your name will be mentioned in any scholarly publications we produce. You (and any family/friends) will also be able to enjoy a private Zoom lecture and Q&A about the Project by the Director.
